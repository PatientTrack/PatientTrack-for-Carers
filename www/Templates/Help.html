PatientTrack for Carers

What is PatientTrack for Carers?

PatientTrack for Carers is an app which is used to help people with Dementia, their carers, and their family members.

What does it do?

This app allows you to locate your patients and/or family members who have the sister app (PatientTrack) installed!

Fantastic! So how do I get started?

Simple! Just fill in the short registration form and proceed to login.
Then just click the Add button and enter the patient's code (available from their settings page) to get their details!
Once you're logged in you'll stay logged in (unless you log out) so you don't need to worry about remembering passwords.
